SESAME Corpus

 

The SESAME corpus for NER in portuguese can be downloaded from google drive: LINK

Notice that we add two new suffixes to the BIO annotation model: "ANOT" and "PRED":

  • ANOT - Specifies that the entity was found by checking for mentions to the known DBpedia entity being mentioned in the article
  • PRED - Specifies that the entity was found by applying an auxiliary parser
